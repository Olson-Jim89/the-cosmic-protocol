§ 00 · HOW TO CREATE YOUR CHARACTER — ATTRIBUTE GENERATION
STEP 1 · BASE

Every character begins with 10 in each of the six Core Attributes — STR, AGI, VIG, GEN, CUN, and AUR. Write 10 in all six boxes before doing anything else.

STEP 2 · BONUS POINTS

You have 5 bonus points to distribute freely. Assign them to any Core Attribute(s) in any combination — you may stack all 5 into one attribute or spread them across several.

STEP 3 · TRADE-OFFS

Choose up to 3 attributes to reduce. Remove any number of points from each chosen attribute (minimum of 1 removed per chosen attribute). Every point removed becomes a free point you may add to any of the six Core Attributes, including the ones you reduced.

EXAMPLE — Start: all 10s. Bonus: +3 STR, +2 AUR → STR 13, AUR 12. Trade-off: remove 2 from AGI and 1 from GEN → gain 3 free points → +2 VIG, +1 CUN. Final: STR 13 · AGI 8 · VIG 12 · GEN 9 · CUN 11 · AUR 12.

There is no upper cap during character creation. Your Race and Profession may grant additional bonuses applied after these steps — see the rulebook for details.
§ 01 · IDENTITY MANIFEST
DESIGNATION

Your character's full name as registered with the Stellar Registry.

RACE / SPECIES

Your biological species — e.g. Human, Veylin, Grohl, Mekavian.

CASTE / RANK

Your social standing, military rank, or faction title. Royalty — Noblesse Oblige: Once per rest, grant +2 to a non-Royal party member's roll (or a Royal of lower status), declared before the roll. Cannot be given to the same party member two sessions in a row. If the blessed roll fails, Noblesse Oblige is unavailable until next rest.

PROFESSION

Your character class — e.g. Soldier, Psi-Tender, Broker, Scout.

§ 02 · CORE ATTRIBUTES — PRIMARY STATISTICS
STR · Strength

Physical power. Governs melee damage and feats of brute force.

AGI · Agility

Speed and precision. Used for ranged attacks, evasion, and stealth. Also determines your Action Points (see § 03).

VIG · Vigor

Endurance and resilience. Determines maximum Health.

GEN · Genius

Intelligence. Covers hacking, medicine, and technical knowledge.

CUN · Cunning

Wits and perception. Used for traps, insight, tracking, and trickery.

AUR · Aura

Force of personality and Psionic potential. Governs persuasion and mental abilities.

ATTRIBUTE MODIFIER

Each point above 10 in any attribute grants +1 to rolls and effects that use it. Each 2 points below 10 apply a −2 penalty. Partial odd-numbered deficits round down.

MODIFIER TABLE  ·  10 = ±0  ·  11 = +1  ·  12 = +2  ·  13 = +3  ·  14 = +4…
9 = −2  ·  8 = −2  ·  7 = −4  ·  6 = −4  ·  5 = −6…
DERIVED — Initiative: turn order roll. Carry Limit: STR×2. Psy Resist: AUR mod. Movement: tiles per action.
§ 03 · COMBAT STATUS — FIELD CONDITION REGISTRY
ACTION POINTS

You gain 1 Action Point for every 5 AGI you have, up to a maximum of 4. Calculate at character creation and update whenever AGI changes. Check a pip when spending an action; clear all pips at the start of your turn.

AP TABLE  ·  AGI 1–4 = 0 AP  ·  AGI 5–9 = 1 AP  ·  AGI 10–14 = 2 AP  ·  AGI 15–19 = 3 AP  ·  AGI 20+ = 4 AP (max)
WOUNDED

Ongoing injury — may impose penalties to rolls per GM ruling.

STUNNED

You lose your next available action. Clear after skipping one action.

PRONE

On the ground. Gain cover vs ranged attacks; melee attacks against you have advantage.

POISONED

Taking ongoing damage each round; see the source item or ability for the amount.

UNTETHERED

Floating in zero-gravity or otherwise unanchored from a surface.

PSY·LOUD

Your Psionic signature is broadcasting and can be detected by sensitive creatures or equipment.

§ 04 · HEALTH & STAMINA — VITALS
HEALTH (green)

Physical hit points. Left box = current HP; right box = maximum HP. Reaching 0 means incapacitation or death.

STAMINA (gold)

Exertion pool spent by feats and special abilities. Left = current; right = maximum. Fully replenishes after a full rest.

Max Health is typically VIG × a class multiplier. Max Stamina is set by your Profession. Consult the rulebook for exact formulas.
§ 05 · COMBAT RATINGS
ATTACK ▸ gold

Your total attack bonus added to weapon rolls when making an offensive action.

DIPLOMATIC ▸ cyan

Your social and negotiation bonus used in non-combat conflicts, persuasion checks, and diplomatic encounters.

DEFENSE ▸ red

The number enemies must meet or exceed on their attack roll to hit you. Includes armor, AGI, and any bonuses.

§ 06 · COMBAT REGISTRY — AUTHORIZED ATTACK PROTOCOLS
ATTACK

Name of the weapon, power, or technique (e.g. Plasma Rifle, Neural Shock).

DESCRIPTION

What the attack does — damage type, special rules, or conditions it inflicts.

RANGE

MELEE · SHORT · MEDIUM · LONG · or a specific tile/meter distance.

DAMAGE

Dice notation for damage rolled — e.g. 2d6, 1d8+STR, 3d4.

ATTRIBUTE

The core stat used for the attack roll — STR for melee, AGI for ranged, AUR for Psionic.

BASH is pre-filled as the universal unarmed strike available to all characters at no cost.
§ 07 · FEAT REGISTRY — SPECIAL ABILITY CODEX
ATTRIBUTE

The core stat this feat scales with (e.g. STR, AUR, GEN). Used to calculate the feat's effect.

FEAT NAME

Full name of the feat as listed in the rulebook. Feats are universal — any character can learn them.

LEVEL

Current rank of this feat. Most feats can be upgraded multiple levels by spending experience. Enter the numeral (1, 2, 3…).

Up to 10 feat slots are available. Leave unused rows blank. Consult Chapter II of the rulebook for the full feat list.
§ 08 · PROFESSION ABILITY REGISTRY — CLASS CODEX
PROFESSION

The class or profession column this ability belongs to — e.g. SOLDIER, PSI-TENDER, BROKER.

ABILITY NAME

Full name of the class ability as listed in your Profession's codex in the rulebook.

LEVEL

Current rank of this class ability. Class abilities unlock and upgrade as your character levels.

Up to 10 slots. Characters with multiple professions may record abilities from each. See Chapter III for profession ability trees.
§ 09 · WORN EQUIPMENT — APPAREL MANIFEST
HEADGEAR

Helmets, visors, tactical goggles, earpieces, hats. Up to 2 items.

TORSO

Jackets, armor vests, tactical rigs, bodysuits. Up to 2 items.

LEGS

Pants, greaves, knee pads, exo-frame legs. Up to 2 items.

FEET

Boots, mag-clamps, jet-skates, anti-grav soles. Up to 2 items.

ARMOR · TIER

The armor category worn: LIGHT, MEDIUM, HEAVY, or EXO-FRAME.

MITIGATION

Flat damage reduction applied to every hit received while wearing this armor.

STEALTH PEN

Penalty applied to stealth checks due to armor bulk and noise. Heavier armor = higher penalty.

§ 10 · GALACTIC CREDIT UNION — CREDIT BALANCE   |   § 11 · CARRIER MANIFEST — BAG CONTENTS
CREDIT BALANCE

Enter your current credit total. 1 cr = 1 standard galactic credit. Deduct when spending at licensed merchants or during downtime transactions.

The credit card number field is decorative — your actual balance is the editable field at the bottom of the card panel.
ITEM SLOTS (×12)

Each of the 12 slots holds one item or a stack of identical consumables (e.g. 3× Ration Bar). Write the item name clearly.

BAG TYPE

The container you're carrying — Standard Pack, Tactical Rig, Void Satchel, Crate, etc.

MAX CARRY WT

Your current carry capacity in kg or units. Cross-reference with Carry Limit in Core Attributes (§ 02).

NOTES

Any relevant bag modifiers, magical properties, or special conditions applied to the container.
